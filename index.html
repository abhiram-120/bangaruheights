About Bangaru Heights

Crafted for comfort, designed for life. Bangaru Heights is more than just an apartment complex; it's a community where modern amenities meet serene living with a clear title and 100% Vasthu compliance.

Experience Elevated Living

Bangaru Heights is a prestigious upcoming residential project located in the vibrant city of Palakolu. We are dedicated to creating homes that blend contemporary architecture with thoughtful design, ensuring a lifestyle of comfort and convenience.

Our project features G+4 floors, offering an exclusive collection of spacious 3 bedroom apartments. Each unit is meticulously planned to maximize space, natural light, and ventilation, providing a perfect sanctuary for you and your family.

Project Specifications

Details of the high-quality materials and features used in Bangaru Heights, ensuring durability and a premium living experience.

STRUCTURE

Reinforced Cement Concrete framed structures.

SUPER STRUCTURE

9"/4½" Red burnt Brick walls in Cement mortars.

MAIN DOOR

Main Door cum Window: Teak Wood frame with Teak Wood Shutter.

OTHER DOORS

Granite Frames & Teak Veneer Doors.

WINDOWS

3 Track UPVC Windows with Matt Glass and Mosquito Mesh.

PLASTERING

Interior Walls & Ceiling with Wallcare Smooth finish.

FLOORING

Good Quality Verified Flooring with 1 metre x 1 metre Tiles.

KITCHEN

Polished Black Granite stone for Platform with Stainless Steel Sink & 2'x4' Ceramic Tiles and dado upto 4' height above the Platform.

WARDROBES

Provision for Wardrobes in all Bed Rooms and Kitchen.

TOILETS & BATHROOMS

Branded Sanitary Ware, Hindware or Jhonson or other branded commodes. Provision for shower with hot and cold water mixing, dado upto 7' height, Two Wash Basins per flat.

ELECTRICAL

Concealed Copper wiring with necessary Points and Switch Boards and provision for Electrical Charging Points in Parking Area, TV and WIFI & Inverter wiring & Home Theater Wiring.

LIFT

Lift with 6 Passenger Capacity.

PLUMBING

Plumbing with standard UPVC & CPVC pipes with concealed pipe-lines for Hot & Cold Water.

PAINTS

Enamel Paint for Wood Work and Grills. Wallcare for inside and outside walls.

WATER

Overhead Storage Tanks for bore well water & Municipal Water.

EXTRAS

Registration Charges, GST, Labour Cess, AP Transco, Generator, Municipal Water Connection Charges and any new Govt. Taxes extra. Any changes or additional works will be charged extra cost.
